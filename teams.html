Professional Teams

There are many professional Ultimate Frisbee teams around the world. The American Ultimate Disc League (AUDL) is the premier professional league in North America, featuring teams from cities across the United States and Canada.

College Teams

College Ultimate is incredibly popular, with hundreds of teams competing at various levels. The USA Ultimate College Championships is one of the most prestigious tournaments in the sport, attracting top teams from across the country.

Club Teams

Club Ultimate is the highest level of competition outside of professional play. Club teams are typically made up of experienced players who compete in regional and national tournaments. The Club Championships is the pinnacle of the club season.

Recreational Leagues

For those looking to play Ultimate Frisbee in a more casual setting, recreational leagues are available in many communities. These leagues are a great way to get involved in the sport, meet new people, and have fun playing Ultimate.
