Watch Your Head

Ultimate Frisbee is a sport that is played with a flying disc. The disc is thrown by players to team members in an effort to score goals. Ultimate Frisbee is a self-officiated sport, meaning that there are no referees. Players are expected to follow the rules and to resolve disputes themselves.

A Sport for Everyone

Ultimate Frisbee is a sport that can be played by people of all ages and skill levels. The sport is easy to learn and can be played in a variety of settings, from backyards to parks to beaches. Ultimate Frisbee is a great way to get exercise, have fun, and make new friends.

The Spirit of the Game

One of the most important aspects of Ultimate Frisbee is the Spirit of the Game. This principle emphasizes sportsmanship, fair play, and respect for opponents. Players are expected to be honest, to treat others with respect, and to put the fun of the game first.
